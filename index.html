Project Overview

Our project aims to address the growing demand for renewable energy integration in modern power systems. Focusing on the utilization of solar and wind energy sources, we have conducted an in-depth analysis to explore the feasibility and potential of these renewable technologies

Abstract

This report presents a comprehensive analysis of the integration and optimization of renewable solar and wind energy sources in modern power systems. Utilizing MATLAB as the primary analytical tool, the study investigates the viability and potential of harnessing solar and wind energy to meet the growing global demand for sustainable electricity generation. The analysis delves into the inherent challenges of variability and intermittency associated with these renewable sources and explores the effectiveness of advanced forecasting techniques to mitigate their impact on grid stability. Additionally, the report assesses the integration of energy storage systems and smart grid technologies to enhance the reliability and efficiency of renewable energy utilization. Findings from the MATLAB-based analysis shed light on the vast untapped potential of solar and wind energy and provide valuable insights for policymakers and stakeholders in advancing the transition towards a cleaner and greener energy future.
